AI-Powered Artificial Heart

Presented by Ritesh Goswami

Introduction

This project aims to develop an AI-powered artificial heart that can monitor, learn, and adapt to heart rhythms. Our device is designed to address the urgent need for innovative solutions in the realm of cardiac care.

Heart diseases are among the leading causes of death worldwide, making advancements in cardiac technology crucial. The integration of artificial intelligence in medical devices offers the potential to enhance patient outcomes through real-time monitoring and adaptive responses to heart conditions.

The motivation behind this project is to provide a solution for individuals with severe heart conditions, ultimately improving their quality of life. With the growing prevalence of heart-related illnesses, our AI-powered heart device will not only save lives but also transform cardiac healthcare.

Technology Overview

The AI chip will detect abnormalities in heart rhythms and make instant adjustments to heart function. By integrating advanced machine learning algorithms, the device will learn from the patient's unique heart patterns, optimizing performance over time.

The architecture of the AI system will utilize neural networks to analyze data collected from the heart in real-time. This system will learn to distinguish between normal and abnormal heart rhythms, enabling it to make informed decisions that enhance patient safety.

Furthermore, the device will be equipped with sensors capable of measuring various physiological parameters such as heart rate, blood pressure, and oxygen saturation. This data will be processed to ensure that the heart operates at optimal levels.

The inclusion of cloud computing will allow for remote monitoring and updates to the AI model, ensuring that the device continues to improve and adapt based on the latest research and data analytics.

Market Need

Heart diseases are a leading cause of death globally. This innovation addresses a critical need by offering a smart solution to heart management, potentially reducing mortality rates significantly.

The demand for advanced cardiac solutions is on the rise as more individuals face heart-related health issues. Our AI-powered artificial heart aims to fulfill this demand by providing a proactive approach to heart health.

Statistics show that nearly 17.9 million people die each year from cardiovascular diseases, accounting for 31% of all global deaths. With an aging population and lifestyle changes leading to increased cases of obesity and diabetes, the need for innovative heart solutions has never been more urgent.

Through targeted market analysis, we have identified key demographics that would benefit from this technology, including patients with chronic heart failure, those awaiting transplant, and individuals with congenital heart defects.

How It Works

The AI-powered artificial heart continuously monitors the patient’s heart activity and adjusts in real-time to prevent cardiac events. It can potentially delay heart attacks by:

  • Detecting early warning signs of irregularities
  • Providing feedback to the patient and healthcare providers
  • Adjusting medication dosages based on heart rhythms

Once implanted, the device will operate using a battery that can be recharged wirelessly. The heart's AI will communicate with the device via Bluetooth, enabling seamless interaction.

The device will also include a backup power supply to ensure functionality in case of unexpected battery failure. This dual-power mechanism enhances reliability, giving patients peace of mind.

Additionally, the AI system will continually learn from each patient’s data, adapting to their unique cardiac patterns and ensuring optimal performance. This is a key differentiator from traditional heart devices, which may not offer personalized adjustments.
